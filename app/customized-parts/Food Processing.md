Stainless steel is the material of choice in the food processing industry due to its unmatched hygiene, corrosion resistance, cleanability, and compliance with global food safety standards. From dairy to meat processing, beverages to confectionery, stainless steel ensures product safety and equipment longevity.

### Why Stainless Steel Dominates Food Processing

1. **Hygienic and Non-Porous**  
   Smooth surface prevents bacterial adhesion.

2. **Food-Grade Compliance**  
   Meets FDA, NSF, EHEDG, and 3-A standards.

3. **Corrosion Resistance**  
   Resists acids, alkalis, and cleaning chemicals.

4. **Thermal Stability**  
   Handles extreme temperatures in cooking and freezing.

5. **Easy to Clean and Sanitize**  
   CIP (Clean-in-Place) compatible.

### Common Grades

- **304 / 304L**: General food contact.
- **316 / 316L**: High-corrosion environments (e.g., salty products).
- **430**: Non-contact parts.
- **Duplex**: High-strength tanks.

### Surface Finishes

- **BA & Mirror**: Internal surfaces.
- **2B**: Structural parts.
- **No.4**: External panels.

### Key Applications

1. **Processing Tanks and Vessels**  
   Storage, mixing, and fermentation tanks.

2. **Piping and Tubing**  
   Sanitary piping systems with welded or clamp fittings.

3. **Conveyors and Belts**  
   Food-grade conveyor frames and belts.

4. **Heat Exchangers and Pasteurizers**  
   Plate and tubular heat exchangers.

5. **Fillers and Packaging Equipment**  
   Filling machines, cappers, and labelers.

6. **Cutting and Slicing Equipment**  
   Blades, tables, and cutting boards.

7. **CIP Systems**  
   Cleaning-in-place tanks and spray balls.

8. **Silos and Bulk Storage**  
   Flour, grain, and ingredient silos.

9. **Dairy Equipment**  
   Milk tanks, cheese vats, and separators.

10. **Meat and Poultry Processing**  
    Slaughter lines, deboning tables, and chilling tunnels.

### Regulatory and Design Considerations

- Smooth welds without crevices.
- Electropolishing for ultra-smooth surfaces.
- Full material traceability and certificates.

### Maintenance

- Daily CIP with approved detergents.
- Periodic passivation.
- Visual inspection for pitting.

Stainless steel ensures food safety, regulatory compliance, and operational efficiency in modern food processing plants.
