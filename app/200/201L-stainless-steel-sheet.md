## What is 201L Stainless Steel Sheet?

**201L stainless steel sheet** is a low-carbon, manganese-substituted austenitic alloy (UNS S20103) that delivers 304-like strength and corrosion resistance at a fraction of the cost. The "L" denotes ≤0.03 % carbon, minimizing carbide precipitation during welding and allowing thinner sections without intergranular corrosion.

## Manufacturing Process & Quality Control

1. **Electric-Arc Furnace Melting** – Scrap and alloying elements melted under argon atmosphere.  
2. **Continuous Casting** – 200 mm thick slabs cast to minimise inclusions.  
3. **Hot Rolling** – Reheat to 1,200 °C and roll to 3–12 mm plate or 2–3 mm transfer bar.  
4. **Cold Rolling** – Transfer bar reduced to 0.5–3 mm at 20 °C for ±0.02 mm tolerance.  
5. **Bright Annealing** – 1,050 °C in hydrogen for mirror BA finish and restored corrosion resistance.  
6. **Finishing** – Skin-pass for 2B, polish lines for No.4/HL, or super-polish for 8K mirror.  
7. **QC Testing** – Spectrometer, ultrasonic thickness, and ASTM tensile tests ensure every sheet meets spec.

## Maintenance & Care Guide

- **Routine Cleaning:** Warm water + mild detergent, soft cloth, rinse and dry.  
- **Fingerprint Removal:** 1 % vinegar solution, wipe along grain, buff with microfiber.  
- **Avoid:** Chloride bleach, HCl, steel wool or abrasive powders.  
- **Storage:** Elevate on wooden pallets, cover with breathable PE film, keep away from moisture and carbon steel dust.  
- **Passivation:** Optional citric acid bath after fabrication to remove free iron and enhance corrosion resistance.
