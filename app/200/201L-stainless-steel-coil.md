## What is 201L Stainless Steel Coil?  
**201L stainless steel coil** is the extra-low-carbon version of grade 201 (≤0.03 % C), minimizing carbide precipitation during welding and severe cold forming. High manganese (5.5–7.5 %) and nitrogen (≤0.25 %) maintain an austenitic structure, giving **201L stainless steel properties** comparable to 304 for urban corrosion, deep drawing and mirror polishing—yet at a fraction of the nickel cost. Compared with 301, 201L offers better corrosion resistance; against 430, it provides non-magnetic, weldable and formable characteristics essential for cookware and appliances.
