## Heat Treatment & Precipitation Hardening Guide

**631 stainless steel pipe** undergoes **solution annealing** at **1040 °C** followed by aging conditions such as **RH950**, **CH900**, **TH1050**, and **TH1100** to achieve customized mechanical properties. The **CH900** condition is recommended for **maximum strength**, achieving a **yield strength** of **900 MPa** and **39–43 HRC** hardness.
