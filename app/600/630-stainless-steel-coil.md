## Heat Treatment & Precipitation Hardening Guide

**630 stainless steel** undergoes **solution annealing** at **1038°C** to dissolve carbides and ensure a uniform structure. It can then be aged in various conditions (**H900**, **H925**, **H1025**, **H1150**, **H1150M**) to achieve the desired balance of **strength** and **hardness**, ranging from **28 HRC** to **48 HRC**, depending on the aging process.
