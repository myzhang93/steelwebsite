## Heat Treatment & Precipitation Hardening Guide

**631 stainless steel** undergoes **solution annealing** at **1040 °C** and can be aged to achieve desired mechanical properties. Aging conditions such as **RH950**, **CH900**, **TH1050**, and **TH1100** can be applied to achieve the appropriate strength and hardness, ranging from **38 HRC** to **48 HRC**.
