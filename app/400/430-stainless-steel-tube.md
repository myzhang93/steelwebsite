## Surface Finish Guide

- **2B**: Standard finish for **430 stainless steel tube**, offering a smooth and slightly reflective surface. Commonly used for **industrial applications**.
- **BA (Bright Annealed)**: A smooth, shiny finish produced by annealing in a controlled atmosphere. Typically used for applications where **aesthetic appeal** is important.
- **No.4**: A brushed finish commonly used in **architectural applications**, giving a consistent, satin appearance with light grain lines.
- **HL (Hairline)**: Features fine lines in one direction, commonly seen in **elevators** and **decorative interior applications**.
- **Mirror 8K**: Achieved through high-quality polishing, providing a **reflective mirror finish**. Ideal for **luxury appliances**, **kitchen equipment**, and **automotive trim**.
