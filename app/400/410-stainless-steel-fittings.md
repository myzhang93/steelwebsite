## Heat Treatment Guide for 410 Stainless Steel Fittings

**410 stainless steel fittings** undergo **heat treatment** for **maximum strength** and **hardness**. **Annealing** is performed at **760–815°C** to improve ductility, while **quenching** at **950–1050°C** followed by **tempering** at **150–650°C** achieves a final hardness of **35–55 HRC**.
