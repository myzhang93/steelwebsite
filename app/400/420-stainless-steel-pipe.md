## Complete Heat Treatment Guide for 420 Stainless Steel Pipe

- **Hardening**: Heat to **980–1050°C**, then quench in oil or air.
- **Tempering**: For tempering, heat to **150–400°C** to achieve a hardness of **48–58 HRC**.
- **Cryogenic Treatment**: Optional cryogenic treatment improves wear resistance and hardness, especially for high-strength applications like **cutting tools** and **surgical instruments**.
