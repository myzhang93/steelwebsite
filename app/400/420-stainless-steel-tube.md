## Complete Heat Treatment Guide for 420 Stainless Steel Tube

- **Hardening**: Heat to 980–1050°C and quench in water or oil to achieve high hardness (up to 58 HRC).
- **Cryogenic Treatment** (optional): This additional step, performed at temperatures below -100°C, further improves wear resistance and hardness.
- **Tempering**: After hardening, temper at 150–400°C to improve toughness and reduce brittleness. Achievable hardness after tempering: 48–58 HRC.
