## Heat Treatment Guide for 410 Stainless Steel Tube

- **Annealing**: Heat to 750–850°C, followed by air cooling. This process ensures maximum ductility and minimizes the risk of cracking.
- **Hardening**: Heat to 950–1050°C and quench in oil or water. This increases the strength and hardness of the material, making it ideal for high-strength applications.
- **Tempering**: Temper at 600–750°C for improved toughness and reduced brittleness. Achievable hardness after tempering is between 22–55 HRC.
