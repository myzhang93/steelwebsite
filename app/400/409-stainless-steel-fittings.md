## High-Temperature & Stabilization Guide

The **Ti-stabilized** and **Nb-stabilized** variants of 409 stainless steel fittings provide enhanced performance under extreme thermal cycling and high-temperature conditions. - **Ti-Stabilized 409**: Best for applications requiring resistance to sensitization and grain growth at elevated temperatures.
- **Nb-Stabilized 409**: Ideal for environments with intermittent exposure to high temperatures, providing enhanced resistance to **intergranular corrosion**.
