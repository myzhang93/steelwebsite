## Heat Treatment Guide for 410 Stainless Steel Plate

1. **Annealing**: 800–900 °C, air cool → ≤22 HRC
2. **Quenching**: 980–1050 °C, oil or air → ≥40 HRC
3. **Tempering**: 200–600 °C → 40–50 HRC (optimal 300 °C)
4. **Post-weld**: Optional for most services; use 309 or 410 filler
